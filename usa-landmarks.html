American Landmarks

Grand Canyon, Statue of Liberty, national parks and more.

Must-See Attractions

The United States is home to some of the world's most impressive natural and man-made landmarks, ranging from ancient natural wonders to modern architectural achievements.

Iconic Landmarks

  • Statue of Liberty: Symbol of freedom in New York Harbor.
  • Grand Canyon: One of the world's most spectacular natural wonders in Arizona.
  • Golden Gate Bridge: Iconic suspension bridge in San Francisco.
  • Mount Rushmore: Four presidential faces carved in South Dakota.
  • Yellowstone: World's first national park with geysers and hot springs.

Natural Wonders

Niagara Falls, Yosemite Valley, the Great Smoky Mountains, and the Rocky Mountains showcase the diverse natural beauty of America.
