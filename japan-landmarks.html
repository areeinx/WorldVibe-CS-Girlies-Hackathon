Japanese Landmarks

Mount Fuji, Kyoto shrines, and modern cityscapes.

Iconic Sites

Japan seamlessly blends ancient temples and shrines with ultra-modern architecture. From sacred mountains to bustling cities, Japan's landmarks reflect centuries of history and innovation.

Must-See Monuments

  • Mount Fuji: Japan's iconic snow-capped volcano and cultural symbol.
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine: Thousands of vermillion torii gates in Kyoto.
  • Kinkaku-ji: The stunning Golden Pavilion temple in Kyoto.
  • Tokyo Skytree: Modern telecommunications tower with panoramic views.
  • Hiroshima Peace Memorial: UNESCO site and solemn reminder of history.

Temples & Shrines

Kyoto alone has over 2,000 temples and shrines. Todai-ji Temple houses a giant bronze Buddha. These sites are centers of spirituality and art, often surrounded by beautiful gardens.
