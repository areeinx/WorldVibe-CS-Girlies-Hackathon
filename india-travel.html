Plan a Visit to India

Travel tips, climate advice and cultural etiquette.

Travel Guide

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers the best weather—cool and dry. The monsoon season (June-September) brings green landscapes but heavy rains. Summer (April-May) is extremely hot.

Getting Around

  • Trains: Extensive network, book well in advance.
  • Flights: Connect major cities; affordable domestic airlines.
  • Auto-Rickshaws: Convenient local transport in cities.
  • Buses: Budget-friendly between cities.

Cultural Tips

  • Remove shoes before entering temples and homes.
  • Use the right hand for eating and greeting (left is considered unclean).
  • Photography may be restricted in religious sites.
  • Dress modestly, especially near temples and in rural areas.
  • Haggling is common in markets and bazaars.
  • Indian rupees are the currency; ATMs are widely available.
