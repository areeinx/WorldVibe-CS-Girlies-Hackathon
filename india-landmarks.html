Indian Landmarks

Taj Mahal, forts, and vibrant city life.

Iconic Sites

India is home to some of humanity's most revered and spectacular monuments. From ancient temples to Mughal architecture, each site tells a story of India's rich history.

Must-See Monuments

  • Taj Mahal: UNESCO World Heritage monument and symbol of eternal love in Agra.
  • Varanasi: Holy city on the Ganges River, sacred to Hindus.
  • Jaipur Palace: Stunning pink architecture, City Palace complex.
  • Ajanta Caves: Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves with intricate murals.
  • Red Fort: Historic Mughal fort in Delhi with impressive architecture.

Temples & Spiritual Sites

The Golden Temple in Amritsar, Meenakshi Temple in Madurai, and temples in Khajuraho showcase India's architectural and spiritual heritage across different regions and faiths.
