France — Overview

Discover the geography and regions of France, from the Riviera to the Alps.

Geography & Regions

France is the largest country in Western Europe, spanning from the Mediterranean Sea to the English Channel. The country features diverse landscapes including the Alps, Pyrenees, and the Atlantic coastline.

Major Regions

  • Île-de-France: Home to Paris, the capital and cultural heart of France.
  • Provence: Known for lavender fields and Mediterranean charm.
  • Côte d'Azur: The glamorous French Riviera with beautiful beaches.
  • Normandy: Famous for its D-Day beaches and apple orchards.
  • Loire Valley: Historic castles and vineyards.
