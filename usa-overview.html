USA — Overview

Explore cities, national parks, and regional cultures.

A Vast and Diverse Nation

The United States is the world's third-largest country by both area and population, spanning from the Atlantic to the Pacific Ocean. It features diverse landscapes, climates, and cultures across its 50 states.

Major Regions

  • Northeast: Historic cities like New York and Boston with colonial heritage.
  • South: Known for warm hospitality, music, and rich history.
  • Midwest: The heartland, famous for agriculture and friendly communities.
  • Southwest: Desert landscapes, canyons, and Native American culture.
  • West Coast: Innovation hubs, mountains, and Pacific beauty.

National Parks

The USA is home to the world's first national park, Yellowstone, and 62 total national parks showcasing America's natural wonders.
