Japanese Culture

Temples, festivals and traditions across the country.

Ancient Traditions

Japanese culture beautifully blends ancient traditions with cutting-edge modernity. Deep respect for tradition and continuous innovation define the Japanese way of life.

Traditional Arts

  • Tea Ceremony (Chanoyu): Ritualized preparation and serving of matcha tea.
  • Kabuki Theatre: Traditional performance art with elaborate costumes and makeup.
  • Noh: Classical Japanese masque theatre, very subtle and refined.
  • Martial Arts: Karate, Judo, Aikido, and Kendo train body and mind.
  • Calligraphy & Painting: Brush painting (sumi-e) and beautiful handwriting.

Festivals & Celebrations

Cherry blossom festivals (Sakura Matsuri), summer fireworks (Hanabi), and New Year celebrations are beloved traditions. Each season has its own festivals and celebrations.
