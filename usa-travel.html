Plan a Visit to the USA

Best times to visit, routes, and travel tips.

Travel Guide

Best Time to Visit

The best time varies by region. Spring and fall offer pleasant weather. Summer is warm but crowded. Winter is cold in the north but mild in the south and ideal for winter sports.

Getting Around

  • Car Rental: Most flexible way to explore, many regions require a car.
  • Flights: Affordable domestic flights connect distant cities.
  • Trains: Amtrak offers scenic routes across the country.
  • Buses: Budget-friendly intercity buses are widely available.

Travel Tips

  • Distances between cities can be vast; plan accordingly.
  • Tipping is customary (15-20% at restaurants).
  • US Visas may be required depending on your nationality.
  • US measurements (miles, pounds, Fahrenheit) differ from metric.
  • Credit cards are widely accepted almost everywhere.
