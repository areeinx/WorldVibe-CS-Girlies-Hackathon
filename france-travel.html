Plan a Visit to France

Tips for travel, seasons, and local customs.

Travel Guide

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April-May) and fall (September-October) offer pleasant weather and fewer crowds. Summer is warm but very touristy, while winter is cold but perfect for holiday markets.

Getting Around

  • Trains: High-speed TGV connects major cities efficiently.
  • Metro: Paris has an excellent subway system.
  • Buses: Affordable and extensive bus networks.
  • Rental Cars: Useful for exploring rural areas and the countryside.

Local Tips

  • Learn basic French phrases—locals appreciate the effort.
  • Meals are leisurely; budget time for dining.
  • Tipping is not mandatory but rounding up is appreciated.
  • Many shops close on Sundays and Mondays.
  • Dress smartly; casual attire is less common than in other countries.
