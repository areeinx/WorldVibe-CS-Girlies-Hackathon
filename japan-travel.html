Plan a Visit to Japan

When to go, how to get around, and cultural tips.

Travel Guide

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March-May) with cherry blossoms and fall (September-November) are ideal. Summer (June-August) is hot and humid, while winter offers skiing and fewer crowds.

Getting Around

  • Shinkansen (Bullet Train): High-speed trains connect major cities efficiently.
  • IC Cards: Suica/Pasmo cards for seamless transit across regions.
  • Metro Systems: Excellent subway in Tokyo, Osaka, and Kyoto.
  • Local Trains: Frequent and punctual regional rail connections.
  • Rental Cars: Option for exploring rural areas and mountains.

Cultural Etiquette

  • Remove shoes before entering temples, homes, and some restaurants.
  • Slurping noodles is acceptable and shows appreciation.
  • Don't tip—it's not customary and may be considered rude.
  • Say "itadakimasu" before meals and "gochisousama" after.
  • Respect quiet on trains and public transportation.
  • Photography restrictions in temples; always ask before photographing people.
