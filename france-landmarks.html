Iconic Landmarks

Iconic sights like the Eiffel Tower and Mont Saint-Michel.

Must-See Attractions

France is home to some of the world's most iconic and visited landmarks, blending history, architecture, and natural beauty.

Famous Sites

  • Eiffel Tower: The symbol of Paris and France, built in 1889.
  • Mont Saint-Michel: A medieval abbey on a tidal island in Normandy.
  • Arc de Triomphe: Monumental arch honoring French victories.
  • Cathedral of Chartres: A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  • Pont du Gard: A stunning Roman aqueduct near Avignon.

Castles (Châteaux)

The Loire Valley is famous for its Renaissance castles. Château de Chambord and Château de Chenonceau are two of the most spectacular examples of French architectural heritage.
