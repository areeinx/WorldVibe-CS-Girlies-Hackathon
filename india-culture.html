Indian Culture

Music, dance, and festivals vary by state and region.

Rich Heritage

Indian culture is one of the world's oldest civilizations with thousands of years of artistic, spiritual, and philosophical traditions. Every state has unique customs and celebrations.

Classical Arts & Dance

  • Bharatanatyam: Classical dance form from Tamil Nadu.
  • Kathak: Storytelling dance form with intricate footwork.
  • Kathakali: Elaborate theater from Kerala with makeup and costumes.
  • Classical Music: Hindustani and Carnatic traditions with ragas.

Festivals

Diwali (Festival of Lights), Holi (Festival of Colors), Navratri, and Eid are celebrated with great enthusiasm. Each region adds its own unique flavor to these festivals.
